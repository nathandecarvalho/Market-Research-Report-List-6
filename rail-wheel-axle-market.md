<p><p>The Rail Wheel Axle market comprises two main types: rolled axles and forged axles. Rolled axles are manufactured through a process of hot or cold rolling, resulting in a cost-effective and uniform product suitable for various rail applications. In contrast, forged axles are created by shaping heated metal under high pressure, providing enhanced strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty rail systems. Both types cater to different requirements in the rail industry, balancing performance and cost.</p></p>
