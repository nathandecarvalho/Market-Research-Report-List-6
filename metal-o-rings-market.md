<p><ul><li>Internal Pressure Non-vented</li><li>External Pressure Vented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal O-Rings market is categorized into two primary types based on pressure applications: Internal Pressure Non-vented and External Pressure Vented. Internal Pressure Non-vented O-Rings are designed to contain fluids or gases under pressure without allowing any escape, making them ideal for applications like hydraulic systems. In contrast, External Pressure Vented O-Rings are constructed to withstand external pressures while allowing for the venting of gases or fluids, often used in environments where pressure differentials need to be managed without leakage.</p></p>
