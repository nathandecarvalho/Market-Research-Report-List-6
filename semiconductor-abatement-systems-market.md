<p><p>The semiconductor abatement systems market includes various technologies for controlling emissions in semiconductor manufacturing. **Combustion type** systems use high temperatures to oxidize volatile organic compounds (VOCs). **Wet type** systems utilize liquid scrubbing solutions to capture pollutants, effective for aggressive chemicals. **Dry type** systems use solid adsorbents to trap harmful substances without water consumption, making them environmentally friendly. **Catalytic type** systems promote chemical reactions at lower temperatures, efficiently converting harmful gases into less toxic substances, enhancing air quality in manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
