<p><p>The axial grain dryers market consists of two main types: single plenum and split plenum systems. Single plenum dryers have a single airflow chamber that circulates heated air through the grain for uniform drying, making them efficient and easy to operate. In contrast, split plenum dryers are designed with two separate air chambers, allowing for adjustable airflow and temperature control, enhancing drying efficiency and flexibility. Both types cater to various agricultural needs, optimizing grain storage and quality.</p></p>
