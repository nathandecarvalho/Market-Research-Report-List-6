<p><p>The SCR denitrification catalyst market is segmented into three main types: honeycomb, plate, and corrugated catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts feature a cylindrical structure with numerous channels, promoting efficient gas flow and high surface area for reactions. Plate catalysts consist of flat surfaces that maximize contact between gases and catalyst materials, suitable for compact applications. Corrugated catalysts, with their wavy structures, enhance gas turbulence and contact, improving efficiency. Each type is tailored to specific operational needs and environmental regulations in denitrification processes.</p></p>
