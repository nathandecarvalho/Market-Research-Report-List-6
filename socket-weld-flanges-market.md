<p><strong>The Socket Weld Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Socket weld flanges are commonly categorized into four main types based on their design and application: </p><p>1. **RF (Raised Face)**: Features a raised section to enhance sealing capability, widely used in high-pressure systems.</p><p>2. **MFM (Male and Female)**: Designed with interlocking profiles for better alignment and strength in various applications.</p><p>3. **TG (Tongue and Groove)**: Offers additional sealing strength through a tongue and groove fit, suitable for high-stress environments.</p><p>4. **RJ (Ring Joint)**: Incorporates a ring joint for a flawless seal under extreme pressure, ideal for oil and gas industries.</p></p>
