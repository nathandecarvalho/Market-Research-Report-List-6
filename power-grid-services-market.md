<p><p>Power Grid Services encompass various market types aimed at ensuring stable and efficient electricity supply. Reactive Power Compensation stabilizes voltage levels by managing reactive power flows. Voltage Regulation maintains consistent voltage levels across the grid. Flicker Control addresses fluctuations in voltage that can cause disturbances. Active Power Filtering eliminates unwanted harmonics, improving power quality. Harmonic Cancellation targets specific harmonic frequencies to prevent distortions and enhance system performance, contributing to a reliable and efficient power grid operation.</p></p>
