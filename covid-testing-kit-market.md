<p><p>The COVID testing kit market is primarily divided into two types: nucleic acid testing (NAT) and antibody testing. Nucleic acid testing, such as PCR tests, detects the presence of the virus's genetic material, enabling early diagnosis of active infections. Antibody testing, on the other hand, identifies antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus, helping determine past infections. Both testing types are crucial for managing the pandemic, guiding public health decisions, and understanding immunity levels in populations.</p></p>
