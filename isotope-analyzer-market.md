<p><p>The isotope analyzer market is divided into two main types: radioisotope analysis and stable isotope analysis. Radioisotope analysis focuses on detecting and quantifying radioactive isotopes, often used in nuclear medicine, environmental monitoring, and radiological assessments. In contrast, stable isotope analysis involves measuring non-radioactive isotopes to study biological processes, climate change, and material provenance. Both types serve critical roles in research, industrial applications, and environmental studies, facilitating a deeper understanding of various scientific phenomena and enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
