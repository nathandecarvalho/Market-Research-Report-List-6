<p><ul><li>Single Polarization Aircraft Weather Radar Systems</li><li>Dual Polarization Aircraft Weather Radar Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Weather Radar Systems market is primarily segmented into two types: Single Polarization and Dual Polarization systems. Single Polarization systems utilize a single electromagnetic wave orientation to detect precipitation and weather patterns, making them simpler and cost-effective for basic weather assessment. In contrast, Dual Polarization systems employ both horizontal and vertical wave orientations, providing enhanced data on precipitation types and size, improving accuracy in detecting severe weather conditions. This differentiation caters to varying operational needs and technological advancements in aviation safety.</p></p>
