<p><ul><li>DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communication)</li><li>Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wireless Automotive Communication Technology market mainly includes Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC) and mesh networks. DSRC is a specialized protocol designed for high-speed vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication, enhancing safety and traffic management through low-latency interactions. In contrast, mesh networks facilitate broader connectivity by allowing vehicles to communicate with one another and directly with nearby devices, creating a decentralized communication environment. Both technologies aim to improve road safety, traffic efficiency, and overall driving experience through real-time data exchange.</p></p>
