<p><p>Digital textile printing machines encompass various printing methods, including sublimation inkjet printing, direct to fabric printing, and direct to garment printing. Sublimation inkjet printing transfers dye onto synthetic fabrics, resulting in vibrant colors and durability. Direct to fabric printing applies ink directly onto textiles, accommodating a wide range of materials and producing high-quality images. Direct to garment printing focuses on printing designs directly onto finished garments, ideal for short runs and intricate patterns, catering primarily to the fashion and custom apparel markets.</p></p>
