<p><p>The silver amalgam alloys market primarily comprises four key components: mercury, silver, tin, and copper. Mercury acts as a binder, facilitating the mixture of the alloy components. Silver enhances strength and corrosion resistance, while tin improves mechanical properties and reduces thermal expansion. Copper contributes to the alloy's hardness and enhances its overall durability. Together, these materials are extensively used in dental applications, particularly for dental fillings, due to their favorable combination of strength, longevity, and biocompatibility.</p></p>
