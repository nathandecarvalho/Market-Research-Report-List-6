<p><p>The High Voltage Power Supply (HVPS) market is categorized into two main types: AC supply and DC supply. AC supply systems convert electrical energy into high-voltage alternating current, commonly used in applications like power transmission and industrial processes. DC supply systems, on the other hand, deliver high-voltage direct current, essential for electrostatic applications, particle accelerators, and various laboratory settings. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient power delivery across diverse sectors, including telecommunications, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
