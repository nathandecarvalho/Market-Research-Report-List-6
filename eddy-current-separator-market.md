<p><p>The Eddy Current Separator market comprises various types including centrifugal, reciprocating, rotary, and diaphragm separators. Centrifugal separators use high-speed rotation to separate materials based on density. Reciprocating separators rely on a back-and-forth motion to enhance separation efficiency. Rotary separators utilize continuous rotation for ongoing material separation. Diaphragm separators employ a flexible membrane to distinguish between materials of different densities. Each type is tailored for specific applications, ensuring effective separation of non-ferrous metals and other materials in recycling and waste management processes.</p></p>
