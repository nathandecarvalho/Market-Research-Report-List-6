<p><p>The orthopedic contract manufacturing market comprises several key types: Implants, Instruments, and Cases and Trays. Implants are devices like joint replacements or fracture fixation systems designed to support or enhance musculoskeletal function. Instruments refer to the specialized tools used during orthopedic surgeries, such as saws and retractors, crucial for precision and efficiency. Cases and Trays are customized packaging solutions that organize and protect surgical instruments and implants, ensuring safe transport and optimal usability in operating rooms.</p></p>
