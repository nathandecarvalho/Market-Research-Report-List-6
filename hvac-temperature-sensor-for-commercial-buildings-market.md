<p><strong>The HVAC Temperature Sensor for Commercial Buildings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NTC Temperature Sensor</li><li>PTC Temperature Sensor</li><li>RTD Temperature Sensor</li><li>Thermocouples (TCs) Temperature Sensor</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVAC temperature sensors play a crucial role in managing climate control in commercial buildings. NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) sensors decrease in resistance as temperature rises, ideal for precision applications. PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors increase resistance with temperature, suitable for protection and self-regulating systems. RTD (Resistance Temperature Detectors) offer high accuracy and stability, often used in industrial settings. Thermocouples (TCs) work across a wide temperature range by measuring voltage differences between two metals. Other types may include infrared and semiconductor sensors, enhancing system performance and efficiency.</p></p>
