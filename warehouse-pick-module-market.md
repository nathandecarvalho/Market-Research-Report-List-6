<p><strong>The Warehouse Pick Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carton Flow Pick Module</li><li>Pallet Flow Pick Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Warehouse pick modules are essential for optimizing order fulfillment in distribution centers. The carton flow pick module utilizes gravity-fed shelving, allowing items in cartons to slide forward for easy access, enhancing picking efficiency. In contrast, the pallet flow pick module employs a similar gravity-based system but accommodates pallets, ensuring a high-density storage solution that improves throughput. Both systems are designed to streamline the picking process, reduce labor costs, and maximize warehouse space, catering to varying inventory and operational requirements.</p></p>
