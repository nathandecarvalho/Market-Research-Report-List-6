<p><p>The solar cell (photovoltaic) equipment market is primarily categorized into three types: single crystal silicon, polycrystalline silicon, and others. Single crystal silicon cells are made from a single crystal structure, offering high efficiency and longevity, making them suitable for space-constrained installations. Polycrystalline silicon cells consist of multiple crystal structures, generally being less expensive but with slightly lower efficiency. The "others" category includes thin-film technologies and emerging materials like cadmium telluride and organic photovoltaics, which cater to specific applications and markets.</p></p>
