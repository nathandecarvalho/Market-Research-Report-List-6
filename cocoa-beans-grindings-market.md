<p><p>The cocoa beans grinding market primarily consists of three main products: cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor is a thick paste made from ground cocoa beans, serving as the base for various chocolate products. Cocoa butter, extracted from the cocoa liquor, is a fatty substance used in chocolate production, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. Cocoa powder, produced by removing most of the cocoa butter from cocoa liquor, is widely utilized in baking, beverages, and desserts, making it a versatile ingredient in the food industry.</p></p>
