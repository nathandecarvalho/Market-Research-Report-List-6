<p><p>The Flip Up Medical Loupes market is primarily characterized by two types: Galilean Lens and Prismatic Lens. Galilean Loupes utilize a simple two-lens system to provide a lightweight and compact design, suitable for general dental and surgical applications. In contrast, Prismatic Loupes employ a complex system of prisms to offer enhanced magnification and reduced distortion, making them ideal for more intricate procedures. Both types are essential in improving precision and ergonomic comfort for healthcare professionals during examination and surgery.</p></p>
