<p><p>The bi-metal clad pipe market comprises two main types: mechanical and metallurgical processes. The mechanical process involves joining two dissimilar metals through methods like welding or bonding, ensuring enhanced corrosion resistance and mechanical properties. The metallurgical process, on the other hand, focuses on integrating metals at a molecular level, typically using techniques like explosive bonding. Both processes aim to produce pipes that combine the strengths of different materials, catering to various industrial applications requiring durability and performance.</p></p>
