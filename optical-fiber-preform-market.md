<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Preform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VAD</li><li>OVD</li><li>PCVD</li><li>MCVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber preform market comprises several key types: VAD (Vapor Axial Deposition), OVD (Outside Vapor Deposition), PCVD (Plasma Chemical Vapor Deposition), and MCVD (Modified Chemical Vapor Deposition). VAD utilizes a vertical deposition method, while OVD employs an external vapor process to create preforms. PCVD combines plasma technology for efficient deposition, and MCVD uses a modified technique involving a rotating tube to form the glass structure. Each method caters to different requirements in optical fiber production, influencing performance and cost.</p></p>
