<p><strong>The High-Speed Optical Interconnects Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed optical interconnects market is divided into single-mode and multi-mode categories. Single-mode fibers utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. Multi-mode fibers contain multiple light paths, which allow for higher data rates over shorter distances, commonly used in local area networks and within buildings. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing distance, bandwidth, and cost considerations for optimal performance.</p></p>
