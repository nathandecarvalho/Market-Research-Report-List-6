<p><p>Piezoceramic components in the industrial market are essential for various applications, categorized primarily into PZT-based, PMN-based, and other materials. PZT-based components, made from lead zirconate titanate, are widely used due to their strong piezoelectric properties and versatility in sensors and actuators. PMN-based components, utilizing lead magnesium niobate, offer enhanced performance, particularly in sensors requiring high sensitivity and low operating voltage. Other materials may include composite and polymer-based piezoelectric options, catering to niche applications with specific performance needs.</p></p>
