The Resistor Partial Voltage Type involves varying resistance levels to relay commands, often used in simpler systems. The Voltage Type utilizes different voltage levels to communicate between the steering wheel controls and the vehicle, enabling more complex functionalities. Impulse Type relies on short electrical pulses for control signals, offering high precision and responsiveness in user inputs, enhancing overall driving experience.</p></p>
