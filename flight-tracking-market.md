<p><p>The flight tracking market consists of several key types: ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance–Broadcast), FANS (Future Air Navigation System), and PFTS (Precision Flight Tracking System). ADS-B enhances situational awareness by broadcasting aircraft position data to ground stations and other aircraft. FANS integrates satellite communication for improved navigation in remote areas, focusing on air traffic management. PFTS emphasizes precise tracking for safety, providing real-time data to enhance operational efficiency and regulatory compliance in both commercial and private aviation sectors.</p></p>
