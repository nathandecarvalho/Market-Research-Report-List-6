<p><p>A Blind Spot Monitor (BSM) is an advanced safety feature in vehicles designed to enhance driver awareness by detecting vehicles in the blind spots during lane changes or merging. It utilizes sensors typically located in the rear bumper to identify vehicles that may not be visible in the side mirrors.
