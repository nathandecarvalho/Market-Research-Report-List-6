<p><p>The Ship-to-Shore (STS) cranes market is categorized into three types: diesel, electric, and hybrid. Diesel cranes are powered by internal combustion engines, offering high mobility and independence from electrical grids, making them suitable for remote areas. Electric cranes rely on grid power, providing efficient and environmentally friendly operations with lower operating costs. Hybrid cranes combine both diesel and electric systems, allowing for flexible operation while minimizing fuel consumption and emissions. Each type serves specific needs within port and cargo handling environments.</p></p>
