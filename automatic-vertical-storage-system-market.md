<p><p>The Automatic Vertical Storage System market is primarily categorized into two types: Automated Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) and Automated Vertical Carousels. VLMs utilize a stack of trays that move vertically to deliver items to an access point, optimizing space and improving retrieval speed. Automated Vertical Carousels consist of rotating shelves on vertical tracks, offering efficient storage and retrieval of products. Both systems enhance storage density and operational efficiency, making them popular in various industries such as manufacturing, retail, and warehousing.</p></p>
