<p><p>Corporate car-sharing markets can be categorized into two types: two-way and one-way. In a two-way market, vehicles are borrowed from a designated location and must be returned to the same place, promoting accountability and efficient usage within a fixed area. In contrast, a one-way market allows users to pick up a vehicle at one location and drop it off at another, offering greater flexibility and convenience. Both models cater to different corporate needs, optimizing transportation resources.</p></p>
