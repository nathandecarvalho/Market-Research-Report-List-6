<p><p>Hot Aisle Containment (HAC) systems are designed to optimize cooling efficiency in data centers by isolating hot air generated by servers. The market is divided into two main types: Horizontal Aisle Containment, which typically uses barriers to enclose the entire aisle at ceiling height, allowing for effective airflow management; and Vertical Aisle Containment, which employs physical partitions to separate hot and cold air while maintaining accessibility. Both systems enhance energy efficiency and cooling performance, catering to varying data center configurations.</p></p>
