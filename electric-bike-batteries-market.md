<p><ul><li>Lead Acid Batteries</li><li>Nickel-cadmium Batteries</li><li>Nickel-metal Hydride Batteries</li><li>Lithium-ion Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bike battery market encompasses several types, each with distinct characteristics. Lead acid batteries are cost-effective but heavy and have a limited lifespan. Nickel-cadmium batteries offer better performance but suffer from memory effect and environmental concerns. Nickel-metal hydride batteries provide improved capacity and are less detrimental to the environment than their cadmium counterparts. Lithium-ion batteries dominate the market due to their lightweight, high energy density, and long life, making them the preferred choice for modern electric bikes.</p></p>
