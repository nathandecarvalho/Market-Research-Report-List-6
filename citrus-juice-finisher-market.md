<p><strong>The Citrus Juice Finisher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-Based</li><li>Paddle Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The citrus juice finisher market is primarily divided into screw-based and paddle-based systems. Screw-based finishers utilize a helical screw mechanism to effectively extract juice and separate solids, offering higher efficiency and continuous operation. In contrast, paddle-based finishers employ rotating paddles to gently mix and process the citrus pulp, ensuring thorough extraction while maintaining juice quality. Both types serve distinct needs in juice production, focusing on efficiency, quality, and scalability, catering to various industry requirements.</p></p>
