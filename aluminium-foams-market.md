<p><p>The aluminium foams market is categorized into Ex-situ Bonded and In-situ Bonded Aluminium Foams. Ex-situ Bonded Aluminium Foams are produced by bonding aluminium particles with a foaming agent before processing, offering controlled density and uniform properties. In contrast, In-situ Bonded Aluminium Foams are created by foaming the aluminium alloy during the manufacturing process, allowing for unique structural characteristics and improved integration with other materials. Both types provide lightweight solutions with excellent performance in various applications, including automotive and construction.</p></p>
