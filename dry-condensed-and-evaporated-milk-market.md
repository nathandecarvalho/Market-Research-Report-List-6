<p><p>Dry, condensed, and evaporated milk are popular dairy products that serve various culinary purposes. Dry milk, or powdered milk, is produced by evaporating liquid milk, removing moisture to provide a shelf-stable product that can be reconstituted with water. Condensed milk is sweetened and thickened by removing most of its water content, making it ideal for desserts and baking. Evaporated milk undergoes a similar process but is unsweetened and retains a creamy texture, often used in savory dishes and recipes that require milk.</p><p>The Dry, Condensed, and Evaporated Milk Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.
