<p><ul><li>Half Bridge Drivers</li><li>Full Bridge Drivers</li><li>Three-Phase Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bridge drivers are crucial in powering electric motors and other loads. Half bridge drivers control a pair of transistors, enabling efficient switching for low to medium power applications. Full bridge drivers manage four transistors, allowing for bidirectional control and higher power outputs. Three-phase drivers are employed in applications requiring three-phase motors, providing smooth operation and better efficiency. Each type serves distinct needs in industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics sectors, enhancing performance in motor control and switching applications.</p></p>
